# Exercise 1 - PL/SQL Control Structures

## Objective

The objective of this exercise is to understand PL/SQL control structures such as loops, conditional processing, and cursor-based iteration.

---

## Scenario 1

Customers above 60 years of age receive a 1% discount on their loan interest rates.

---

## Scenario 2

Customers having a balance greater than $10,000 are promoted to VIP status.

---

## Scenario 3

Customers whose loans are due within the next 30 days receive reminder messages.

---

## Concepts Used

* FOR LOOP
* UPDATE statement
* COMMIT
* DBMS_OUTPUT.PUT_LINE
* Date comparison using SYSDATE
* Cursor FOR Loop

---

## Output

* Interest rates updated for eligible customers.
* VIP status assigned to eligible customers.
* Reminder messages displayed for loans due within the next 30 days.

---

## Conclusion

This exercise demonstrates how PL/SQL control structures can automate repetitive banking operations using loops, conditional logic, and database updates.
